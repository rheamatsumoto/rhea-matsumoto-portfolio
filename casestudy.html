Space Satellite Systems

October 2025 - March 2026

This is a project I worked on within a design Cohort team at Include, a student organisation at UC Davis. Our objective was to re-design the interface of the website belonging to the student organisation “Space and Satellite Systems” (SSS) to maximise their outreach to interested students and sponsors.

Step 1: Define

Project Overview

Client: Space and Satellite Systems (SSS) is an undergraduate-led organisation at UC Davis that provides students with hands-on space technology engineering experiences. SSS provides an inclusive environment where students can make meaningful contributions to projects and learn industry skills.

"How Might We" Statement: How might we design the SSS website to be more welcoming for both students and sponsors while improving access to the most important information?

Our Goals

Step 2: Research

Competitive Analysis

We compared similar websites with SSS' goal and highlighted and discovered features that were effective and ineffective.

User Personas

We created four user personas to guide our design process and help us understand the primary users of the club’s website.

User Flows

Based on the User Personas, we created User Flows to help us understand what users needed from the site and how they currently get there.

Step 3: Ideate

Low Fidelity

I sketched the interface for the "Join Us" and "Sponsor Us" page.

The "Join Us" page establishes a welcoming and inviting environment to appeal to interested students. The page will include a community photo, contact information, a Google calendar, and employers that hired alumni.

The "Sponsor Us" page encourages potential sponsors to endorse their organisation. The page will include a link to their sponsor packet, a description explaining how sponsorship goes towards supporting students in the organisation, and a list of sponsors who have endorsed the club.

Mid-Fidelity

Following my sketches, I designed monochrome wireframes with placeholder text in Figma.

We created a design system that aligned with SSS's request for a professional design that embodies the aesthetic of space.

Upon recieving feedback on my monochrome wireframes, I established more consistency towards text alignment and shapes and added text boxes to highlight significant information.

High Fidelity

We recieved feedback from SSS and incorporated it into our high fidelity, final designs. They wanted to see more white space and a simple format with iconography.
